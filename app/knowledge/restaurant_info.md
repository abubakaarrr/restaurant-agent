# La Casa Restaurant — General Information

## About Us

La Casa Restaurant is a warm, family-friendly restaurant in the heart of the city, serving modern Mediterranean and international cuisine. We've been proudly serving our community since 2015.

**Address**: 42 Harbour Street, City Centre (near Central Station)
**Phone**: +1 (555) 234-5678
**Email**: hello@lacasarestaurant.com
**Website**: www.lacasarestaurant.com

## Parking

- Street parking available on Harbour Street and adjacent streets (metered, free after 6 PM)
- Nearest car park: City Centre Parking Garage, 50m away (entrance on Market Street)
- Ride-share drop-off zone directly in front of the restaurant

## Getting Here

- **Train**: 3-minute walk from Central Station (Exit B)
- **Bus**: Routes 12, 15, and 22 stop on Harbour Street
- **Bike**: Bike racks available outside

## Facilities & Accessibility

- Fully wheelchair accessible (ramp entrance, accessible bathrooms)
- High chairs available for young children — just ask on arrival
- Free Wi-Fi for all guests (network: LaCasa_Guest, password: lacasa2024)
- Air-conditioned indoor dining and covered outdoor patio
- Private dining room available for events (seats up to 16 people)

## Private Events & Functions

We host birthdays, corporate dinners, anniversary dinners, and private events.
- Private room seats up to 16 guests
- Custom menus available for groups
- Enquire via email or phone for availability and packages

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do you take walk-ins?**
Yes, walk-ins are welcome based on availability. Reservations are recommended for Friday and Saturday evenings.

**Is there a dress code?**
Smart casual. We ask that guests avoid beachwear or sportswear in the dining room.

**Can I bring a birthday cake?**
Absolutely. Let us know in advance and we'll keep it in our fridge. A plating fee of $5 per person applies.

**Do you have a kids' menu?**
Yes, we offer a children's menu for guests under 12. Ask your server.

**Can you accommodate large groups?**
Groups up to 12 can be booked over the phone. For groups of 13 or more, please contact us via email or phone directly — a set menu may be required.

**Do you cater for dietary restrictions?**
Yes — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and halal options are available. Please inform us of any allergies when booking.

**Is there a corkage fee?**
We do not allow BYO at this time.

**Do you offer takeaway or delivery?**
Takeaway is available for collection. We do not currently offer delivery.

**Can I modify or cancel my booking?**
Yes — please call us directly to modify or cancel. We require at least 2 hours' notice for cancellations.
